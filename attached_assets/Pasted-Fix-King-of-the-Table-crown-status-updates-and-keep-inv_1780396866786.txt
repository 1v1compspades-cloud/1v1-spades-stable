Fix King of the Table crown/status updates and keep invite link visible while waiting.

Issues:
1. Crowns/status are not updating clearly on the win screen and waiting screen after a King of the Table match.
2. The invite/challenger link disappears when the King is waiting for people to join.

Goal:
King of the Table should clearly show who the current King is, show crown/winner status correctly after every match, and always keep a challenger invite link available while waiting.

Requirements:
1. Do not change normal 1v1 gameplay logic.
2. Do not change bidding, scoring, cards, sockets, timers, tournament logic, or tournament advancement.
3. Fix only King of the Table UI/state flow.
4. After a KOTT match ends, winner should immediately become/stay King.
5. Winner should see:
   - Crown icon / King label
   - “You are King”
   - “Waiting for challenger”
   - Copy Challenger Link button
6. Loser should see:
   - Rejoin Queue
   - Back to King of the Table Lobby
   - Leave Table / Home
7. Waiting screen should always show:
   - Current King name
   - Crown/King status
   - “Waiting for challenger”
   - Copy Challenger Link button
8. The challenger/invite link must remain visible and usable whenever the table is waiting for a challenger.
9. The invite link must not disappear after a match completes.
10. The invite/challenger link must stay token-free and must not include hostToken, adminToken, playerToken, or private session tokens.
11. Spectator view should update to show the current King waiting for challenger.
12. If there is no challenger in queue, do not close the lobby. Keep King waiting with invite link available.
13. If challenger joins, update state to “Challenger joined” / “Match in progress.”
14. Test both cases:
   - King wins and remains King
   - Challenger wins and becomes new King

Acceptance test:
- Create King of the Table lobby.
- Player A joins as King.
- Confirm waiting screen shows crown/King label and Copy Challenger Link.
- Player B joins as challenger.
- Finish match with Player A winning.
- Confirm Player A sees crown + “You are King — waiting for challenger” + Copy Challenger Link.
- Confirm Player B sees Rejoin Queue / Back to KOTT Lobby / Leave.
- Finish another match with challenger winning.
- Confirm challenger becomes King and sees crown + waiting screen + Copy Challenger Link.
- Confirm spectator view shows the correct current King.
- Confirm invite link never contains host/admin/private tokens.
