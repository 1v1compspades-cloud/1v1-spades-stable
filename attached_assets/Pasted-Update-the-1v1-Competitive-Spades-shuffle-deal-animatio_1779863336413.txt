Update the 1v1 Competitive Spades shuffle/deal animation to use the same visual structure as 2v2 spades.

Goal:
In 1v1 mode, the deal animation should show 4 piles being dealt like a normal 2v2 spades table, then clearly show the two side hands being discarded.

Requirements:

1. Use 4 visual pile positions during the shuffle/deal animation:
   - Left side pile
   - Opponent/top pile
   - Right side pile
   - Dealer/bottom pile

2. Keep the shuffle and cut animation on the dealer’s side.
   - The dealer should visually control the shuffle.
   - The cut should still appear near the dealer/deck area.

3. Deal the cards around the table in normal spades order:
   - Start dealing to the left pile
   - Then top/opponent pile
   - Then right pile
   - Then bottom/dealer pile
   - Repeat until all cards are dealt

4. After the 4 piles are dealt, show that the side hands are discarded:
   - Left pile and right pile should briefly glow/fade/dim
   - Add a label over each side pile that says “Discarded”
   - Then animate those two piles sliding/fading away into a discard zone
   - Do not reveal the actual cards in those side piles

5. The playable hands should remain:
   - Top/opponent pile
   - Bottom/dealer pile

6. Add a small text explanation during the discard moment:
   “Side hands discarded — 1v1 hands locked in”

7. Do not change actual game logic unless needed.
   - This is mostly a visual animation update.
   - The real hands should still match the existing 1v1 deal logic.
   - Side/discard hands should stay hidden by default.

8. Make sure this works on mobile.
   - Do not let the animation cover bidding buttons, player cards, score, or trick area.
   - Use compact labels and smooth fade/slide animations.

9. Keep the 2v2 shuffle animation unchanged.
   - Only apply the discard-side-hands visual behavior when game mode is 1v1.

Expected result:
The player sees a normal 4-pile spades deal, understands that the left and right side hands are discarded, and then the match continues with only the top and bottom hands active.
