Core Rules

  • Use a 24-card deck: 9, 10, J, Q, K, A in each suit.
  • The Jack of trump is the right bower and is the highest card.
  • The Jack of the same-color suit is the left bower and is the second-highest trump.
  • The left bower counts as trump for follow-suit logic.
  • Trump can be led immediately after trump is chosen.
  • Players must follow the led suit when possible.
  • The maker scores 1 point for 3 or 4 tricks.
  • The maker scores 2 points for all 5 tricks.
  • The defender scores 2 points when the maker is euchred.
  • First to 10 points wins by default.
  • Stick the Dealer is ON by default.

Fair Play

  • This site is free-play only and is built for practice, friend matches, and tester events.
  • Real-value play is not part of this site.
  • Host controls are for event recovery, disconnects, and tester support.
  • Spectators can view public match status, but hidden hands stay private.
