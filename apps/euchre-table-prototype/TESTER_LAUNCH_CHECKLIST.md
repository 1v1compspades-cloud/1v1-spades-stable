# Tester Launch Checklist

## Play-A-Friend

- Open `https://1v1euchre.com`.
- Create a room.
- Copy the room code or room link.
- Join as Player 2 from a second browser/device.
- Choose trump.
- Verify right bower is highest trump.
- Verify left bower counts as trump.
- Verify trump may be led immediately.
- Verify legal play follows the led suit when possible.
- Play a full hand.
- Complete a match to 10.
- Refresh a player page and confirm the seat is kept.
- Open the room as a spectator and confirm hidden hands stay private.

## Tournament

- Create a tournament.
- Save the private host key.
- Confirm players only receive the public tournament link/code.
- Join 4 players.
- Verify the player list and open slots.
- Verify the host can start the bracket with the private host key.
- Open each match link.
- Complete or report a match result.
- Confirm the winner advances.
- Complete the final.
- Confirm champion display appears.
- Confirm completed matches remain visible.

## Mobile

- Open home, room, rules, and tournament pages on a phone-sized viewport.
- Confirm buttons are large enough to tap.
- Confirm room code and tournament code are readable.
- Confirm current turn, trump, score, and match status are visible.
- Confirm there is no horizontal page overflow.

## Safety

- Confirm the site says it is free-play only.
- Confirm the launch disclaimer says: No cash games. No deposits. No wallets. No paid entries.
- Confirm hidden hands are not visible to spectators.
- Confirm the private host key is never shown on player or spectator pages.
- Confirm host controls stay hidden until a valid private host key is entered.
